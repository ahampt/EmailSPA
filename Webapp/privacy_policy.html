How EmailSPA uses personal information:

The information you give EmailSPA is absolutely safe. EmailSPA will never rent, sell, share, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties. The information given to EmailSPA is never sent to nor stored in any server as all operations are on the client. This is the equivalent to just typing things into a text document on your computer as the data is never transmitted over the network. It is just routed around in your browser to the javascript file that you download on access to the site.
